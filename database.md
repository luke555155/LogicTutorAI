## 第 1 題 【單選題】

**English:** What is the capital of France?

**中文：** 法國的首都是哪裡？

**選項：**
- A. London
- B. Paris
- C. Berlin
- D. Madrid

**正確答案：B**

**題目解析**

Paris is the capital and largest city of France. It is located in the north-central part of the country on the Seine River.

法國的首都是巴黎，位於國家的北中部，賽納河上。

## 第 2 題 【多選題】

**English:** Which of the following are European countries?

**中文：** 以下哪些是歐洲國家？

**選項：**
- A. Spain
- B. Japan
- C. Germany
- D. Australia

**正確答案：AC**

**題目解析**

Spain and Germany are both European countries. Japan is in Asia, and Australia is in Oceania.

西班牙和德國都是歐洲國家。日本在亞洲，澳大利亞在大洋洲。
